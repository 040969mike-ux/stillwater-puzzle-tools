# Stillwater Puzzle Tools

Free online puzzle helpers — no app, no login, no download required.

🔗 **[stillwaterpuzzletools.com](https://stillwaterpuzzletools.com)**

## Tools

- **[Crossword Helper](https://stillwaterpuzzletools.com/crossword-helper.html)** — Upload a photo of your crossword, click any clue when stuck. AI solves that one clue only.
- **[Word Search Solver](https://stillwaterpuzzletools.com/word-search-solver.html)** — Upload a photo of your word search. Click any word to reveal where it's hiding in the grid.
- **[Hidden Word Finder](https://stillwaterpuzzletools.com/hidden-word-finder.html)** — Find words hiding inside a string of letters.
- **[Sudoku Tools](https://stillwaterpuzzletools.com/sudoku.html)** — Solve, hint, and validate any Sudoku puzzle.
- **[Word Descrambler](https://stillwaterpuzzletools.com/word-descrambler.html)** — Unscramble any word and get definitions.
