About Us

Built for Puzzle Lovers.

Stillwater Puzzle Tools is a free collection of helpers for people who love working puzzles the old-fashioned way — with a pencil, a cup of coffee, and no wifi required.

Our Story

Stillwater started with a simple observation: millions of people work crosswords, word searches, and Sudoku puzzles every single day — in books, newspapers, and magazines — and sometimes they just get stuck.

Not stuck enough to give up. Just stuck enough to need a little nudge. A hint. A quick check. Something to get them moving again without spoiling the whole puzzle or sending them down a rabbit hole of unhelpful search results.

So we built the tools we wished existed. Free, fast, and focused. No accounts to create. No apps to download. No paywalls. Just help, right when you need it.

"No wifi required."

That's our philosophy in three words. The best puzzles are offline. The best moments are unplugged. We just make sure you're never completely on your own when you hit a wall.
