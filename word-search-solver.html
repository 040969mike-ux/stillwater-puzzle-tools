How to get your puzzle image
📱 On your phone
  1. Open your puzzle book
  2. Tap the camera icon below
  3. Take a photo of the puzzle page
  4. Select the photo from your gallery
Tip: Lay the book flat, shoot straight down, good lighting
💻 On a laptop or computer
  1. Take a photo with your phone
  2. Email the photo to yourself
  3. Open the email on your computer
  4. Save the photo to your Downloads folder
  5. Click the upload button below and select it
Tip: Or take a screenshot if using a PDF puzzle book

Tips for best results

  1. Screenshots work better than photos — no angle or lighting issues
  2. For photos, lay the book flat and shoot straight down
  3. Good lighting makes a big difference — avoid shadows
  4. After upload, verify the grid before searching
  5. Fix any wrong letters in the verify box — then confirm
  6. Click a word only when you're stuck — it highlights instantly
